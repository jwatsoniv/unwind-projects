IMPORTANT NOTES FOR CLEANING TEAM

• Full/double beds: Queen sheets are used on all full and double-size beds. Counts in the Queen column already include these.
• Sofa sleepers: All sofa sleeper sheets are included in the Queen count.
• Bunk beds: Each bunk = 2 mattresses. Twin counts reflect individual mattresses. Some bunks have a full bottom (queen sheets) and twin top — both counted accordingly.
• Half baths (x.5): Half baths do not get towels or amenity kits — only full baths.
• Towels per bathroom: Pack per company standard — confirm with Aftin if unsure.
• Questions? Contact Aftin before heading to property — do not guess on linen counts.
